About Us

We are a Christian ministry based in Alberta, Canada​ with a focus of serving newcomers to our country​ from a Middle Eastern Muslim background. Many o​f these beautiful people who have come to our cou​ntry as refugees have experienced trauma and loss p​rior to arriving here. We take seriously the Biblical man​date to "love the alien as yourself, for you were aliens in t​he land of Egypt: I am the Lord your God" (Leviticus 19:​34). We do this practically through helping these ne​wcomers navigate new life in Canada, and through teachi​ng them English, through our "Adopt a Refugee Progr​am." We are also intentional about engaging these n​ewcomer friends in matters of Christian faith, within the con​text of respectful relationship.

Resources

One aspect of our vision is to train and mobilize the Body of Christ to respectfully serve and engage with the Muslim communities in our Canadian urban centers. One way that we do this is through seminars and training venues.


Our most in depth training venue is the "Understanding Islam" Class, which is a 12 hour interactive learning opportunity, where we explore topics such as:

  • The origins of Islam
  • What Islam teaches about Jesus
  • What Muslims believe and practice
  • How the Qur'an was compiled/written
  • Do Muslims and Christians worship the same God?
  • Serving your Middle Eastern neighbor
  • Contextual tools to respectfully engage your Muslim friend

APPRENTICESHIP

Get hands on experience and an in depth learning opportunity through our 9 month Apprenticeship Program.

You will be immersed in serving a growing Middle Eastern community in a Canadian urban center, while developing a framework for ministry to Muslims...without leaving Canada.

There are four components to the Apprenticeship Program:

  1. Immersion in serving and ministering to this Middle Eastern newcomer community
  2. Academic learning on Islamic worldview from a biblical lens
  3. Hands-on experience as a missionary in training in Canada
  4. Mentorship from an experienced worker

If you are interested in learning more about the Apprenticeship, please contact us.
